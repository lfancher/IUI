Eris

Eris is the Greek goddess of chaos, discord, and strife. She's also my cat. Let's just say I named her a little bit too well. Self-fulfilling prophecies and all that.
She is the reason I only have one animal. She accepts no others in her space. This includes 99% of humans on the planet as well. Eris likes me, my mother, my brother, and my girlfriend. I think she'd be pleased if no one else ever deigned to occupy her space ever again. And she lets you know her displeasure through hisses, swats, and growls.
But, as pictured, she's also adorable and that's why she gets away with being a hellion.

This is the picture Eris would send to the modeling agency. She probably thinks I could have done a little better at photographing her majesty, but it's better than looking like a shapeless void like in other images.
This expression highlights how she believes she is a goddess amongst mere mortals. We are beneath her.
